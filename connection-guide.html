👤 STEP 1 — Applicant Information

Provide your full name, email address, and phone number. Make sure all details are accurate as they will be used for your application record.

🎓 STEP 2 — Pre-Membership Education Seminar (PMES)

Attendance is required before applying. The seminar is free and a Certificate of Attendance will be issued upon completion.

STEP 3 — Prepare & Upload Required Documents

Download, fill out, and sign each applicable form. You will upload these during the online application.

💡 Upload all required documents through the Apply Connection portal. Make sure each file is clear and readable before submitting. Physical copies must also be brought to the NORECO I Main Office, Tinaogan, Bindoy, Negros Oriental on the day of your scheduled inspection.

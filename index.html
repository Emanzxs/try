About NORECO I

Negros Oriental I Electric Cooperative (NORECO I) provides reliable electricity services to communities in Bindoy, Negros Oriental. The ElectService system improves efficiency, transparency, and accessibility for all residents — integrating service requests, permit processing, installation scheduling, and billing into one modern platform.
